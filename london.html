The Palace of Westminster

Home to the Houses of Parliament, the Palace of Westminster is a magnificent example of Gothic Revival architecture. Take a tour of the Houses of Parliament to see the historic chambers, or marvel at the beauty of Big Ben, the iconic clock tower.

Kew Gardens

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Kew Gardens is a sprawling botanical garden with a vast collection of plants from around the world. Explore the Temperate House, the world's largest Victorian glasshouse, or take a walk through the beautiful Bamboo Garden.

Hyde Park

One of London's largest parks, Hyde Park offers a welcome escape from the city bustle. Stroll along the walkways, rent a rowing boat on the Serpentine Lake, or explore the variety of gardens within the park, including the Rose Garden and the Diana, Princess of Wales Memorial Garden.
