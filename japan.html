Tokyo

Japan's capital city is a must-visit for any first-time visitor to Japan. Tokyo is a sprawling metropolis with something to offer everyone, from neon-lit skyscrapers and ancient temples to world-class museums and delicious food. Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji is a dormant volcano that is considered to be a sacred mountain in Japan. Mount Fuji is a popular destination for hiking and climbing, and the views from the summit are truly spectacular.

The Japanese Alps

The Japanese Alps are a mountain range that stretches across central Honshu, the main island of Japan. The Japanese Alps are a great place to go hiking, camping, and skiing.
