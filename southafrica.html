Blyde River Canyon

This natural wonder is the world's third-largest canyon and is known for its dramatic cliffs, waterfalls, and rock formations. Visitors can go hiking, biking, or boating in the canyon..

The Garden Route

This scenic stretch of coastline is known for its beautiful beaches, lagoons, and forests. Visitors can go swimming, sunbathing, hiking, or whale watching in the Garden Route.

Cape Town

Consistently ranked as one of the most beautiful cities in the world, Cape Town offers a combination of stunning natural beauty and vibrant culture. Table Mountain, a flat-topped mountain that overlooks the city, is a must-see. Visitors can hike or take a cable car to the top for panoramic views. The city also has beautiful beaches, world-class restaurants, and a variety of historical and cultural attractions.
