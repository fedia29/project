Sheikh Zayed Road

This major highway is lined with some of Dubai's most impressive skyscrapers, including the Burj Khalifa. Take a drive down Sheikh Zayed Road to see the city's glittering skyline at night.

Dubai Miracle Garden

This massive flower garden is a riot of color, with over 50 million flowers and plants arranged in a variety of shapes and designs. It's a photographer's paradise!

Dubai Creek

This historic waterway is the heart of old Dubai. Take a traditional abra (wooden boat) ride along the creek to see the traditional dhow (sailing boat) harbor and the Al Fahidi Historical District.
