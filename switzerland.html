Lake Geneva

The largest lake in Switzerland, Lake Geneva is a popular destination for swimming, boating, and sailing. The city of Geneva is located on the shores of the lake and is a great place to base yourself for exploring the region.

Lucerne

Lucerne is another charming Swiss city with a beautiful old town. The city is famous for its Chapel Bridge, a wooden bridge that is decorated with paintings depicting scenes from Swiss history..

Basel

Basel is a major center for art and culture. The city is home to a number of world-class museums, including the Kunstmuseum Basel and the Fondation Beyeler.
