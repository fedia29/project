Iguazu Falls

Located on the border between Brazil and Argentina, Iguazu Falls is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most spectacular waterfalls in the world. The falls consist of 275 individual waterfalls cascading down a dramatic gorge, creating a breathtaking sight.

Lençóis Maranhenses National Park

This unique national park is characterized by vast white sand dunes that resemble a giant desert. Dotting the landscape are crystal-clear lagoons, creating a surreal and breathtaking scene. Explore the park on foot, take a 4x4 ride, or go sandboarding down the dunes.

Paraty

This charming colonial town is located on the Costa Verde, south of Rio de Janeiro. Explore the well-preserved historic center with its cobblestone streets and colorful houses, go sailing in the beautiful bay, or hike in the surrounding rainforest.
